Nelson Mandela

A global symbol of peace, justice, and resilience

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid activist and politician who served as President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999. He is remembered as one of the most influential leaders of the 20th century.

Key Milestones

  • 1918: Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  • 1962: Imprisoned for anti-apartheid activities
  • 1990: Released after 27 years in prison
  • 1994: Elected as South Africa’s first Black president
  • 2013: Passed away at the age of 95
